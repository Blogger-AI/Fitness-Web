Full Body Workout

This full body workout is designed to engage multiple muscle groups, perfect for anyone looking to build strength and endurance.

Exercises:

  • Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Planks: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Cardio Blast

Get your heart pumping with this dynamic cardio workout designed to boost your stamina and cardiovascular health.

Exercises:

  • Jump Rope: 5 minutes
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Strength Training for Beginners

This beginner-friendly strength training routine focuses on building foundational strength using bodyweight and light weights.

Exercises:

  • Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Chest Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Overhead Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
